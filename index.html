🇦🇺 AUSTRALIAN INVESTOR NOTE
AUD/USD amplifies your returns at rotation. Your Balanced Indexed and High Growth Indexed funds hold unhedged international equities. When the SPX crashes, the AUD typically falls simultaneously (risk-off). This means your fund falls less in AUD terms than the raw SPX drop implies. At recovery, you benefit from both equity appreciation AND AUD appreciation — a double tailwind. A falling AUD at the rotation point is therefore a green signal specific to your situation as an Australian investor. This is why AUD/USD is included as an indicator (weight 7%) and why the effective SPX entry threshold in AUD terms may be slightly lower than the 20% hard gate suggests.
